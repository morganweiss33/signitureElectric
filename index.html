Safety

Signature Electric is deeply committed to ensuring a safe working environment during construction and go far beyond the industry standards.

    Employee Safety

  • Reviews all aspects of the Corporate Safety Manual
  • Includes handout of Field Safety Book which are reviewd with each employee prior to employment.
  • Orientation repeated every three years
  • Weekly "Tool Box Talks" are held to ensure that employees are constantly reminded about safety regulations and procedures while on the job
  • OSHA 30 Certified Foremen
  • Silica Training
  • Confined Space Training
  • Aerial Lift Training
  • Drug-Free Workforce

    Project Specific Safety Procedures:

  • Create and review the project specific 3SP plan
  • Create a JHA for all project tasks
  • Covers each task to be completed daily by foreman and crew
  • Identifies each hazard & necessary action to mitigate the hazard
  • Requires signature of each crew member
  • Contains post inspection checklist
